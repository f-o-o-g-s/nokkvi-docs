The Genres view lists every genre in your library with album and song counts. A 3×3 collage of album covers stands in for genre artwork.

For navigation, search, multi-select, ratings, and shared right-click actions, see [Library Basics](/guides/library-basics/). For artwork panel sizing, see [Artwork & Performance](/guides/artwork/#the-artwork-panel).

## Sort modes

- **Name** *(default)*, **Album Count**, **Song Count**, **Random**

## Columns

Toggle via the gear icon: **Multi-select**, **Index**, **Thumbnail**, **Album Count**, and **Song Count**. Everything but **Multi-select** defaults on. The Thumbnail toggle also controls whether nested child album rows in the genre→album expansion render their artwork.

## Expansion

`Shift + Enter` on a genre expands its albums inline. You can also click the "*N* albums" text on a genre row to focus and expand it directly. Only one genre can be expanded at a time; changing sort or search clears the expansion.

## Search

Search matches genre name only (unlike most other views, which use Navidrome's full-text index).

## Click-through expansion

From an expanded album row:

- "*N* songs" text or `Shift + Enter` on a centered child album → Albums view with that album expanded inline at the top.
- **Album** name → Albums view with that album expanded inline at the top.
- **Artist** name → Artists view with that artist expanded inline at the top.

Collapse with `Shift + Enter`, or refresh / change sort to clear the expansion state.
