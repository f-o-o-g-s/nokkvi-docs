title: Genres

---

The Genres view lists every genre in your library with album and song counts. A 3×3 collage of album covers stands in for genre artwork.

For navigation, search, multi-select, ratings, and shared right-click actions, see [Library Basics](/guides/library-basics/).

## Sort modes

- **Name** *(default)*, **Album Count**, **Song Count**, **Random**

## Two-level expansion

`Shift + Enter` on a genre expands its albums inline. `Shift + Enter` on an expanded album (or clicking the "*N* songs" text) expands that album's tracks. Only one genre and one album within it can be expanded at a time. You can also click the "*N* albums" text on a genre row to focus and expand it directly.

## Search

Search matches genre name only (unlike most other views, which use Navidrome's full-text index).

## Click-through filters

From an expanded album or track row:

- **Album** name → Albums view filtered to that album
- **Artist** name → Artists view filtered to that artist
